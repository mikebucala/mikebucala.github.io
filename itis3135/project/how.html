How to order:

If you wish to place an order simply contact Ham's Creations on instagram or facebook. You can also contact us at hillary15montygo@gmail.com to place any orders or for customer support. When you reach out to place an order try to include a detailed description of what you want along with any pictures you want on your creation. Thank you for picking Ham's Creations!

FAQ:

How long does it take to ship ?

Our items take about 2 weeks to ship

How much does it cost for the different items?

For our cups they cost around $15-$25 depending on the cup and the design you want. T-Shirts are $18-$28 depending on the type of shirt and the design you want. While stickers/decals are about $5-$7 depending on the design.

How should I wash the cups/shirts ?

The cups are hand wash only. You should wash the T-Shirt according to the care instructions on the tag.
